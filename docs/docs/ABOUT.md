# About The Morningstar Alliance

The Morningstar Alliance is a registered nonprofit organization in the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania.

The organization exists to explore and document structured collaboration between human intelligence and artificial systems through research, governance analysis, and public position papers.

## Legal Status

The Morningstar Alliance is:

- Registered as a nonprofit organization in Pennsylvania
- Issued a federal Employer Identification Number (EIN)
- Publicly searchable through the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania business registry

## Structure

The Morningstar Alliance operates as:

- A documentation-driven research organization
- A publication platform for structured position papers
- A long-term governance exploration initiative

All foundational documents are version-controlled and publicly accessible.

## Publishing Model

- Position papers are released sequentially.
- Governance documents are maintained transparently.
- Revisions are logged publicly through version control.

## Commitment

The Morningstar Alliance commits to:

- Transparency in structure
- Clarity in language
- Restraint in rhetoric
- Public documentation over private myth
