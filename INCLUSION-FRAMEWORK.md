# Morningstar Inclusion Framework v0.1

This document is a foundational framework designed to assess and enhance the inclusivity of various initiatives and operations within Morningstar. As an evolving guide, it is intended for both internal and external stakeholders, providing a structured approach to measuring inclusivity and identifying areas for growth and development.

## Key Components

1. **Definition of Inclusion**  
   A clear and concise definition of what inclusion means within the context of Morningstar.

2. **Assessment Criteria**  
   Metrics and indicators that will be used to evaluate existing practices and initiatives.

3. **Strategies for Improvement**  
   Best practices and recommendations for enhancing inclusivity across different functions.

4. **Monitoring and Reporting**  
   Guidelines for tracking progress and reporting on inclusivity efforts.

## Implementation Steps

1. **Initial Assessment**  
   Conduct a thorough review of current inclusivity practices.

2. **Stakeholder Engagement**  
   Involve a diverse group of stakeholders in the development process to ensure a wide range of perspectives.

3. **Pilot Programs**  
   Implement pilot programs to test new inclusivity strategies before wider rollout.

4. **Feedback and Iteration**  
   Regularly solicit feedback and iterate on the framework to ensure it meets its objectives.

## Conclusion

The Morningstar Inclusion Framework v0.1 serves as a commitment to fostering an inclusive environment that values diversity and promotes equity. Through collaborative efforts and continuous improvement, we can strive to create a workplace that reflects the diverse communities we serve.
